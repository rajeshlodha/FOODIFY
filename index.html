Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

+

Most orders are delivered within 20-45 minutes depending on the restaurant and your location. You can track your order in real-time using our app.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit and debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay, PhonePe), UPI, and cash on delivery for selected locations.

Can I cancel my order?

+

Yes, you can cancel your order within 30 seconds of placing it. After that, contact our customer support and we'll help you if the restaurant hasn't started preparing.

Is my personal information safe?

+

Absolutely! We use state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. Your data is never shared with third parties.

What if my food arrives late or damaged?

+

We offer a full refund or replacement if your food arrives late or damaged. Simply report it through the app and our team will resolve it within 24 hours.

Do you have vegetarian options?

+

Yes! We have 100+ vegetarian restaurants and every restaurant has clearly marked vegetarian options. You can filter by dietary preferences while browsing.
